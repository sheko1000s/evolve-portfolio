A modern programming education brand with a clear tech-driven identity, designed to feel fresh, motivating, and professional for learners.

Project Brief

Up Skilling is an education brand in the programming and technology field. The project focused on building a strong digital identity that feels modern, clear, and inspiring. The visual direction aimed to present the brand in a clean and engaging way that speaks to learners, reflects growth, and supports a premium educational image.
